Welcome to the Social Credit System

Welcome to our Social Credit Score Service, where your digital footprint shapes your reputation in the online community. We believe in fostering a positive and respectful environment, rewarding users for their constructive contributions and adherence to community guidelines.

Criteria for Each Credit Score Range

0-300 (Critical Improvement Needed):

  • Frequent negative interactions
  • Limited community engagement
  • Consistent violation of community guidelines

301-500 (Below Average):

  • Some positive interactions but overshadowed by negatives
  • Moderate community engagement
  • Occasional violation of community guidelines

501-700 (Average):

  • Balanced mix of positive and neutral interactions
  • Regular community engagement
  • Generally compliant with community guidelines

701-850 (Above Average):

  • Mostly positive interactions
  • Active and positive community engagement
  • Consistent adherence to community guidelines

851-950 (Excellent):

  • Overwhelmingly positive interactions
  • High-level community engagement with valuable contributions
  • Strict adherence to community guidelines

951-1000 (Outstanding):

  • Exceptional positive interactions
  • Exceptional community engagement with substantial contributions
  • Exemplary adherence to community guidelines
